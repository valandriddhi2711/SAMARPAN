Reduce Wastage of Needy Items

Help others without any reason and give without the expectation of receiving anything in return. Always give without remembering and always receive without forgetting.

Why this matters

  • Social impact: Fulfills immediate needs for those who cannot afford essentials.
  • Environmental impact: Keeps useful items out of landfills.
  • Economic value: Extends the life of goods and reduces unnecessary purchases.

What you can donate

  • Clothes
  • Footwear
  • Books
  • Toys
  • Furniture
  • Vessels

How Samarpan handles donations

  1. Collection: Scheduled pickups and drop-off centers.
  2. Processing: Inspection, cleaning, and minor repairs.
  3. Distribution: Shared with verified NGOs, shelters, and community programs based on need.

Donor checklist

  • Wash or clean clothes before donating.
  • Pack items by category and label sizes where applicable.
  • Do not donate expired or open food packages.
  • Include a short note if an item has special instructions.

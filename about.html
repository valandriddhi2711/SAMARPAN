About Us

Samarpan provides a platform for people to donate unused but usable items to help the underprivileged. Our system ensures that donated goods reach those who need them most.

How to Donate:
  • Select the category (e.g., clothes, books, toys).
  • Fill out the donation form with details.
  • Schedule pickup or drop off the items.
  • We collect, verify, and distribute the items.
Accepted Items:
  • Clothes
  • Footwear
  • Books
  • Toys
  • Furniture
  • Vessels
  • Non-perishable food items
  • Small working appliances

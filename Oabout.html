About Organ Donation

Organ donation is the process of giving healthy organs or tissues to help patients suffering from organ failure. It includes heart, kidney, liver, and more.

Key Aspects

  • Save up to 8 lives
  • Living & deceased donation
  • Safe and legal process
  • High demand worldwide

How to Become a Donor

  • Register officially
  • Inform your family
  • Carry donor card

Why Students Matter

Young people play a key role in spreading awareness and encouraging organ donation.
